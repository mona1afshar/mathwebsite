After reading through the mini lesson you can give the questions a try.

Questions:

1. Mona is participating in the Olympics, where she must swim in a pool that is 50 meters long. It takes her 30 seconds to swim one length (from one side to the other), and when t = 48 seconds, she is swimming towards the north side of the pool, 5 m away from the south side. How far from the south side of the pool did she start?

2. Suraiya is selling her soul to the devil for virtual KPOP event tickets. However, her ritual handbook stated that she must give two equivalent equations as an offering first. Using the facts that:

  1. The gap between the highest and lowest number of ticket buyers is 3 days
  2. The lowest number of buyers is 2,000 people.
  3. The highest number of buyers is 4,500 people.
  4. During day 0, there were 4,500 buyers.

Calculate the two equivalent equations of sine and cosine.

Once both questions have been answered, put the answers into the end of the link like so:
