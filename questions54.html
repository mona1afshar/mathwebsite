A trigonometric Identity is an equation involving trigonometric ratios. To prove a trigonometric identity you must prove that both sides of the equation are equal.

Lois is out at the park with Mona and Suraiya flying her brand new paw patrol kite. When it’s Lois’ turn she flies the kite on level ground, and the string forms an angle of 60° with the ground. Suraiya and Mona are standing some distance from Lois and see the kite at angles of elevation of 70° and 25°, respectively. Mona is 10 m from Lois. For each question below, round all possible answers to the nearest metre (no decimal).

  1. How high is the kite above the ground?
  2. How long is the string?
  3. How far is Suraiya from Lois?

When complete add the sums from part 1, 2 and 3 and input it into the link

For example if your total sum was 20 it would look like this: https://mona1afshar.github.io/mathwebsite/questions20.html
