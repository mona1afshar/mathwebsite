After reading through the clue you can give the question a try.

Question:
The function y= f(x) has been transformed to y=af [k(x-d)]+c. Determine the sum of a, k, d, and c in each of the following translations

  1. Vertical compression by the factor 3 , reflection in the y-axis, and translation 4 units right, applied to y=x
  2. Vertical stretch by the factor 4, reflection in the x-axis, translation 1 units left, and translation 6 units up, applied to y=1x

When complete add the sums received in questions 1 and 2, then input them into the link.
For example if your total sum was 20 it would look like this: https://mona1afshar.github.io/mathwebsite/questions20.html
