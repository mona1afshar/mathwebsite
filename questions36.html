1. Two sides of triangle ABC have the equal lengths of 3y(6x2 + y). The perimeter of triangle ABC is 40x2y + 6y2 + x2. Triangle DEF is three times the size of triangle ABC and has the same side length ratios. What is the length of side DF? What is the perimeter of triangle DEF?

2. Simplify/Expand the following expression and state any restrictions on the variables.

Once the questions have been answered, put the answers into the end of the link like so:
              [length of side DF (multiply a and b values] x [simplified numerator] x [simplified denominator]

Ex.     Length of side DF = 10x2+3y = 30x2y
            Expanded numerator = a
            Expanded denominator = 50
            -> 30x2y x 10a x 50 = 15000x2ya
