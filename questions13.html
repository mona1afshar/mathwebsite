Question:

A company is opening a new office. The initial expense to set up the office is $10 000, and the company will spend another $2500 each month in utilities until the new office opens.

  1. Write the equation that represents the company’s total expenses in terms of months until the office opens.
  2. What is the average rate of change in the company’s expenses from m=3 to m=6
  3. Calculate the instantaneous rate of change at m=2

Once complete, add up your answers from part 2 and 3, round up to the nearest whole number, then input them into the link.
